What is Have a Chat Seat?

Have a Chat Seat is an initiative for people who are feeling lonely and need someone to talk to.

Our Mission

We believe in the power of conversation to bring people together. Our goal is to provide a safe and welcoming space where individuals can connect with others, share their thoughts, and feel heard.
